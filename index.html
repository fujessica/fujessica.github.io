━━━━━━ REASONS TO JOIN BURNSIDE ━━━━━━

We are very happy

Burnside highschool is one of the happiest places on earth and if you ask any of the students there, they will agree. Most students at Burnside Highschool enjoy learning and discovering more about the courses they chose for the year because of the excellent resources provided and the generally positive attitude. The students here are all extrememly positive people and that spreads to other studetns creating an over all positive atmosphere.

Clubs

Burnside highschool hosts a large variety of clubs such as programming club, debate club, esports club and much more. With such a wide variety of clubs to join a community is never too hard to find, this means that friends are easily found and that no one will have nowhere to sit at lunchtimes. Clubs are open to all students and are typically held in one of the various classrooms at lunch.

Motivated students

Students at Burnside highschol are extrememly motivated in the work they do because of the healthy work envionment provided. Students can turn to their peers who are more than happy to see what they can do and if they don't watn to they can just ask their highly competent teachers. The community at Burnside is highly welcoming meanign rich learning is bound to happen because students can look to many different sources for help.

Passionate teachers

The teachers at Burnside all tend to understand their subjects very well and provide quality education for the students to ensure that they succeed in life later on. They care about so much they will talk to them and ask about how their day was. Other than a specific science and history teacher that is the case with most teachers at Burnside.

Great facilities

At Burnside highschool we have a wide array of facilities that allow students to utilise the school to its full potential. We have a pool for students to use in the summer to splash around with their friends. A huge library can be found in the main area with all the classics such as Harry Potter, Percy Jackson and Turtles all the way down.

Course selection

If there is specific course our students want to study, it will most definitely be available at Burnside highschool. We have one of the biggest selction of courses in New Zealand and many field trips around the school year. In year 12, geography students take an exciting trip to Mount Cook to explore its landscape and natural resources. In year 11, the extension classes take an interesting trip to the University of Cantebury for a look into radioactiviity.

Music and Sports

Burnside Highschool has some of the best sports teams in the whole of New Zealand. Our senior girls volleyball team won the New Zealand Secondary Schools Volleyball Chaampionship in 2021 and our esports team won the Meta Highschool Championship in 2023. The music program at Burnside highschool is also very impressive as there are many different groups and activities to join such as Orchestra, Big Band, Jazz SMP and more. Burnside highschool also provides free music lessons to those in the music program so learning an instrument is provided by the school.
